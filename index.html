Privacy Policy

1. Data Collection

We collect personal information such as name, email address, phone number, and location when you register on Eternal Global Network. This data is used solely for account management and service delivery.

2. Data Usage

Your data is never sold to third parties. We use it to provide platform services, send important notifications, and improve user experience.

3. Payment Security

All payment information is encrypted. We do not store full card details. Transactions are processed through certified payment providers.

4. Cookies

We use cookies to keep you logged in and improve performance. You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal data at any time by contacting our support team.
